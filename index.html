IP&TT

Intellectual Property & Technology Transfer

This identity for the VIT IP&TT Cell is built on the pillars of innovation, protection, and growth. The logo visualizes the journey from a nascent idea to a protected, impactful creation. It represents the fusion of knowledge (the book) with bright ideas (the lightbulb brain), all shielded and nurtured by the institution (the crest). The design is modern, scalable, and professional, reflecting the forward-thinking mission of the cell.
